About Disease Prediction System

The Disease Prediction System (DPS) is an intelligent web-based platform designed to assist users in identifying potential diseases based on their symptoms. By utilizing cutting-edge machine learning algorithms trained on diverse medical datasets, DPS offers users fast and informative health predictions.

The core idea behind DPS is to provide individuals with a helpful first step in understanding possible health conditions before visiting a medical professional. While it does not replace expert consultation, DPS serves as a valuable tool for early awareness and health monitoring.

With a simple and user-friendly interface, DPS allows users to enter symptoms manually or upload bulk symptom data through CSV reports. Each prediction is generated by an optimized machine learning model that has been trained and validated for accuracy.
